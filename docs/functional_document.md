### Project Overview

Art Venue is an online platform that bridges the gap between artists and venue owners. It allows venue owners to list their venues and discover talented artists who can perform at their spaces. Venue owners can create events. At the same time, artists can create profiles, and apply to perform at premium venues. The platform simplifies collaboration, saves time, and promotes growth by bringing both parties together in one digital ecosystem.

### Key Features

**Artist Profiles:** Artists can list thier profiles.   
**Venue Listings:** Venue owners can list their venue and it's details.  
**Collaboration System:** Artists can apply to perform at events.  
**RBAC:** Role based access system for artist and venue owners.  
**Creation of PWA:** Creation of progressive web apps.

### Modules

**Authenitcation and Authorization Module:** Secured User authentication and authorization module with the help of JWT tokens.  
**Artist Module:** Artist can create their profiles and list themselves. They can also update thier information.  
**Venue Owners Module:** Venue owners can list thier venues and after listing thier venues they can list events. Each event will have payout, venue, current status, etc.  
**Event Module:** Events can be created by venue owners and artist choose to perform on it.
